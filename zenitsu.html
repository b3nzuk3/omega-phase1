Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu Agatsuma (我妻 善逸) is one of the three deuteragonists (alongside Nezuko and Inosuke) of the 2016 fantasy horror anime and manga series Demon Slayer.Zenitsu is a powerful Demon Slayer and a member of the Demon Slayer Corps. Although he was originally roped into joining the Corps by his mentor Jigoro Kuwajima, Zenitsu later realizes the importance of the Demon Slayer Corps.
